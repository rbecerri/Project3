What is Malware?

Malware is any software designed with the intent to inflict damage to a computer, server, client, or computer network after being introduced in some way into a target's computer. The code is described as computer viruses, worms, Trojan horses, ransomware, spyware, adware, and scareware, among other terms.

Malware Industry.

The malware industry is valued at over a billion dollars and continues to be a well-organized, well-funded market dedicated to evading traditional security measures.
